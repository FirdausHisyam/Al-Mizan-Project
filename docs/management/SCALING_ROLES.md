# Organizational Structure for Global Scale

To scale the **Al-Mizan Project** to the size of a tech giant like Google, while maintaining its core mission of Islamic knowledge unification, we would need a specialized organizational structure. This structure blends high-tech engineering with deep theological scholarship.

## 1. Executive Leadership (C-Suite)

* **CEO (Chief Executive Officer)**: Visionary leader bridging technology and Islamic scholarship.
* **CTO (Chief Technology Officer)**: Oversees the global technical strategy, distributed systems, and AI direction.
* **CRO (Chief Research Officer - Theological)**: A high-ranking scholar ensuring the integrity of the knowledge graph.
* **CPO (Chief Product Officer)**: Defines the roadmap for consumer apps, APIs, and educational tools.
* **CSO (Chief Security Officer)**: Critical for protecting the integrity of sacred texts and user data.

## 2. Engineering & Technology

### Core Infrastructure (The "Google Spanner" Team)

* **Distinguished Engineers (Distributed Systems)**: Building the custom graph database engine if SurrealDB hits limits.
* **Rust Core Developers**: Optimizing the low-level query engine and memory safety.
* **SRE (Site Reliability Engineers)**: Ensuring 99.999% uptime for global access.
* **Security Engineers**: Cryptography experts ensuring data immutability.

### Artificial Intelligence & NLP

* **Chief AI Scientist**: Leading research in Classical Arabic NLP.
* **NLP Researchers (Classical Arabic)**: Specializing in 7th-century Arabic grammar and semantics.
* **Graph Neural Network (GNN) Specialists**: Developing algorithms to predict and verify abrogation (Naskh) chains.
* **Computational Linguists**: Mapping semantic relationships between Hadith and Quran.

### Frontend & Experience

* **Principal UI Engineers**: Architects for high-performance rendering (WebAssembly/WebGL).
* **Data Visualization Experts**: Specializing in rendering massive graphs (millions of nodes) in the browser.
* **Accessibility (A11y) Engineers**: Ensuring the platform is usable by the visually impaired and global audience.

## 3. Theological Research Board (The "Content" Core)

* **Head of Sharia Board**: Final authority on theological disputes within the graph.
* **Muhaddithin (Hadith Scholars)**: Teams dedicated to verifying Isnad (chains of transmission) for millions of Hadith.
* **Mufassirin (Quran Exegetes)**: Scholars mapping Tafsir relationships.
* **Digital Philologists**: Experts in digitizing manuscripts and verifying textual accuracy.

## 4. Product & Design

* **Product Managers (Education)**: Building tools for universities and Madrasas.
* **Product Managers (API)**: Managing the "Islamic Knowledge Graph API" for third-party developers.
* **UX Researchers**: Studying how scholars and laypeople interact with religious texts digitally.
* **Information Architects**: Designing the ontology of the knowledge graph.

## 5. Operations & Legal

* **Global Policy Lead**: Managing compliance with digital laws across different countries.
* **Islamic Law Compliance Officer**: Ensuring the platform's financial and operational practices adhere to Sharia.
* **Localization Teams**: Translating the interface and content into 100+ languages.
* **University Partnership Managers**: Collaborating with Al-Azhar, Madinah University, etc.

## 6. Data & Analytics

* **Data Engineers**: Building pipelines to ingest petabytes of scanned manuscripts and audio.
* **Knowledge Graph Ontologists**: Defining the schema for the "Tawhidic" data model.
